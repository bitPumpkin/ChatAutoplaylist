GoodGame Chat AutoPlaylist


The application parses GoodGame's channel chat for YouTube links and adds corresponding videos to the playlist.
Links must be adressed to the streamer to be captured and transmitted. The application requires authorization
to a Google Account and link to the GoodGame channel to work with. It's absolutely free for any use and distributed under
